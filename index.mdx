## What is Mallory?

Mallory is a cybersecurity intelligence platform that helps you stay ahead of emerging threats. It aggregates, analyzes, and presents threat intelligence from thousands of sources using AI and automation.

Mallory supports:

- **Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)** – Understand threat actors, campaigns, and TTPs
- **Security Operations (SOC)** – Use indicators and context for detection and response
- **Risk & Vulnerability Management (VM, CTEM, GRC)** – Track vulnerabilities, exploitations, and exposure
