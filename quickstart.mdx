## Get started with Mallory

Follow these steps to start using Mallory.

### Step 1: Sign in or sign up

Access Mallory at [app.mallory.ai](https://app.mallory.ai). Sign in with your account or create one if you're new.

### Step 2: Explore current events

Open **Current Events** to see the latest threat intelligenceâ€”curated and summarized from open sources. Use this to stay on top of new threats and vulnerabilities.

### Step 3: Try Chat

Use **Chat** to ask questions in natural language. You can query entities, relationships, and recent activity to investigate threats and generate insights.

### Step 4: Browse threat entities

Go to **Intelligence** (or **Threat entities**) to search for threat actors, malware, vulnerabilities, and organizations. Each entity page shows details, aliases, and related intelligence.
